Project Background

Many 4th grade students struggle with learning how to apply the four core mathematical operations to problems introduced in their studies. Kiwi: The Mathematical Mage seeks to address these struggles by providing students a way to practice 4th grade math problems through gameplay. By gamifying the learning experience, our project will motivate students to practice their math skills and have fun doing so.

Project Description

Protect your clutch of eggs from waves of hungry predators in this magical math adventure made for 4th graders. Choose your favorite math challenge such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, or division, and use your problem-solving skills to charge your spells. Earn mana by answering questions, cast powerful magic to stop the invaders, and collect berries to upgrade your abilities. With colorful visuals, whimsical music, and an easy-to-read interface, Kiwi: The Mathematical Mage makes mastering math fun, magical, and exciting. How many waves can you survive?
